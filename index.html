About Me

My journey began with a foundation in Business Management at KCA University, where I developed a strong understanding of organizational dynamics and strategic thinking. This business acumen naturally led me to explore the digital landscape, where I discovered my passion for creating user-centered products.

At Moringa School, I immersed myself in Product Design principles, learning to balance user needs with business objectives. Complementing this with Digital Marketing certification from HubSpot, I've developed a holistic approach to digital product development - from initial concept through to market implementation.

My experience at the Ministry of East Africa Community provided valuable insight into public sector challenges and the importance of creating solutions that serve diverse communities. I'm passionate about leveraging design and technology to create positive impact.
